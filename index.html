Text Properties


Text style - a style attribute used to add styles.

Example: I am normal, I am red, I am blue, I am big.



Additional, you can mix and match the font, size, color, background color, and text alignment to make your text more presentable.

Example: I am stylized


Text decoration - has other properties that changes color, style, and thickness.

Example:

I am decorized element,

Also me


Text transform - has properties that changes the text to uppercase, lowercase, and capitalize.

Example: I am uppercase. I am lowercase. I am capitalize. I am none. (transform to none).


Text-indent - Always remember to put a dash (-) between text and transformation. so that your CSS properties will work.
